Roti

Roti It is a famous dish of India. People love to eat Roti because it is tasty as well as healthy if made correctly. Beside that is doesn't require much ingredients to enjoy this dish

Required Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Little bit of ghee

Steps to make

  1. Take some wheat flour and make dough adding some salt to it
  2. Not take some dough and flatten it
  3. Now heat a pan
  4. Now bake it at mid flame when it turns brownish take it off the pan and let it inflate on gas stove
  5. Now take half or less tablespoon of ghee and spread on one side
  6. Tda! Roti is ready now enjoy with any vegetable
